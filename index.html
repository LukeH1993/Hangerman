Hangerman

Hangerman is a hungry and angry stickman who needs your help to fill in the missing letters to complete the correct word, which will be an item of food. If you get the word wrong, hangerman will gradually appear on the screen and you will lose letters. If you get the word right, you will be a step closer to winning the round. Once you have finished the word you will win the round and can play another round. If you play easy, you will have 60 seconds to complete the word. If you play hard, you will have 30 seconds to complete the game. Happy guessing!
